Description

Give your bed a touch of modernistic appeal with our Artiss Bed Frame. Wrapped in premium faux leather or linen fabric, the bed frame is at once smooth to the touch and luxurious all round. The headboard provides ample space for a releasing back rest and you will surely appreciate the high density foam that fills the headboard and every other inch of the bed frame for a well-padded look and feel. Within the frame, a highly flexible wooden arched slat base allows the bed to flex properly for undistributed sleep. An adjustable centre support beam keeps the bed steady and provides additional central support to the structure. Not least , the sturdy steel frame is also reinforced with steel bars and held together by heavy-duty metal connectors for enhanced stability. Available in single, king single, double, queen and king sizes in three pleasant colours.

Note: Mattress is sold separately

Note: VANKE-Non gas lift bed /WARE-Gas lift storage bed
